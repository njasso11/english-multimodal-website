Note-Taking Strategies

Effective notes help you understand and retain information, not just copy it.

  • Focus on key ideas instead of full sentences
  • Use headings and bullet points for structure
  • Highlight important terms and definitions
  • Rewrite notes after class to reinforce learning
Tip: Don’t wait until exams—review notes within 24 hours of class.

Creating a Study System

A strong note system helps you stay organized across multiple classes.

  • Use separate notebooks or folders per subject
  • Color-code topics or chapters
  • Keep digital backups if possible
  • Organize notes weekly instead of all at once

Why it matters: Organization reduces stress and saves time when studying for exams.
